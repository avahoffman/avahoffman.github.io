Research

Scientific Publications

Blue grama genetic diversity

Colorado State University

Does history and climate shaped genetics in a drought tolerant grass? Do plant genetics play a part in the food chain? I used the widespread grass blue grama to reveal important genetic variation in American prairies.

February 2017 - February 2019

Nonlinear plasticity

Colorado State University

When droughts get worse, do strains of grass respond similarly? I used big bluestem grass to show how worsening drought can lead to different stress thresholds, even within a small population of plants.

May 2014 - February 2017

Gene expression & drought

Colorado State University

Do similar kinds of grass have different gene regulation? I showed how two common grasses have different molecular "strategies" for coping with droughts.

May 2014 - February 2017
